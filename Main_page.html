ABOUT US

This platform is designed to bring together tennis ball cricket players from every corner of India, offering a space where talent from even the smallest villages can be showcased on a national level. Whether you're playing in local gully matches or competing in city tournaments, this platform empowers players to connect, compete, and rise to new heights. It provides an opportunity for every cricket enthusiast to create profiles, share records, join teams, and take part in tournaments nationwide. No matter where you start, this is where every player has the chance to shine, compete, and conquer.
